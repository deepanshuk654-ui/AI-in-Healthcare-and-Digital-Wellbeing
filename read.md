AI Digital Wellbeing & Healthcare Assistant

AI Digital Wellbeing & Healthcare Assistant is a web application that helps users track lifestyle habits and monitor health using AI-based analysis. The platform provides health insights, stress detection, symptom checking, and personalized recommendations.

Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
Backend: Python Flask
Database: SQLite (MySQL compatible)
Charts: Chart.js
AI Vision: MediaPipe

Features

User authentication (Signup / Login)
Health data tracking (sleep, screen time, mood, exercise, water, work hours)
AI health score and personalized recommendations
AI chatbot for health advice
AI camera stress detection
AI symptom disease checker
Health analytics dashboard with charts
Health history tracking
